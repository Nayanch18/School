campus life

Kids Dhamaal

Called Kids' Dhamaal, the Kids’ Carnival is one of the biggest events of the academic year at EPS, with proceeds going towards helping the needy in our community. A number of stalls had been put up, selling attractive jewellry and handicrafts. Exciting games of skill and chance were conducted and there was a variety of stalls serving mouth-watering delicacies. Face painting, mehendi and nail art stalls were crowd-pullers. All the stalls were beautifully decorated and arranged along the perimeter of the playground, leaving the center open for the people to move around

children’s day celebration

Children are love personified. Children’s Day is when we express our love for them. Nov 14th, 2019, was a day filled with laughter, fun and frolic for our students. During a special morning assembly, teachers performed for the students in order to express their unconditional love for them.The students were overwhelmed with the entire effort and gave standing ovations. The campus was buzzing with excitement and joy as students received a wrist band as a token of love. The day ended with beautiful memories lingering in everybody’s minds.

Decennial Sports Meet

A much-awaited event, the Decennial Sports Meet at EPS . The formal events included a march past, flag hosting, inauguration with the release of pigeons, administration of the Oath of Sportsmanship, and the lighting of the Olympic torch. The performances of mass drill, Zumba, Yoga and formation dance followed and were spellbinding.. All in all, the Senior Sports Day was a beautifully choreographed event that cheered the heart of every student, teacher and parent!
